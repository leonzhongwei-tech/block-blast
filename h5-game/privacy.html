Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to provide and improve our game service:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, screen resolution.
  • Usage Data: Game progress, scores, levels completed, session duration.
  • Advertising Identifiers: Click IDs from advertising partners for attribution purposes.
  • IP Address: Used solely for language localization and approximate geographic location.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game service.
  • To personalize your experience (language, region).
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve game performance.
  • To serve relevant advertisements through our advertising partners.
  • To attribute advertising conversions to our marketing partners.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics / Google Tag Manager: For usage analytics and conversion tracking.
  • MGID: For displaying advertisements within the game.
  • Affise / MeviusAds: For advertising attribution and performance measurement.

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to save your game progress, preferences, and advertising identifiers. No personal login or account information is stored.

5. Data Retention

Game progress data is stored locally on your device. Analytics data is retained by our third-party providers according to their respective retention policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Our game is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can clear all locally stored data by clearing your browser's site data for this domain.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@blockblastmini.com
